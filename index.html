Who am I?

Hi, I'm Oliver Bota. A passionate Front-end developer based in Sweden.

I am exploring new opportunities and strongly believe that my experience and education can contribute to creating value.

I possess strong communication skills in both Swedish and English, both spoken and written. My computer skills include the use of Windows, Linux,
and all common Office programs. Beyond general IT knowledge, I have obtained IT certifications where I learned about IT support, administration,
troubleshooting, network technology, security, and other areas relevant to a digitized workplace.

Previously, I studied political science, administrative law, and legal sociology, subjects that have given me a broad understanding of political
processes and legal concepts. My education has equipped me with great skills relevant to the position I am seeking.

As an individual, I am responsible, structured, and meticulous. I thrive on routine in my daily life and have developed good teamwork skills through
my previous workplaces . I have a strong work ethic and can work both independently and in a team, valuing a positive and professional approach towards others.
